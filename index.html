Below are samples of documentation that I created while working at Infor, a global leader in cloud-based, industry-specific enterprise software. My main focus was Infor Distribution SX.e and Infor CloudSuite Distribution, the on-premises and cloud versions of ERP distribution software for small-to-medium businesses. I was also the principal writer for the Infor Distribution Storeroom application, a warehouse management tool that enables distributors to manage inventory at customer locations.

Unless otherwise noted, I used Oxygen XML Editor within a Madcap IXIA DITA CCMS to originally create the documentation and then published it to an online library. Except for graphics and internal job aids, I applied the global English writing style to the documentation.

Administration Guide

The Infor Distribution SX.e Administration Guide is a system administration guide for the maintenance and configuration of on-premises Distribution SX.e after an installation or upgrade. I wrote this guide in collaboration with the Senior Principal Architect of Distribution SX.e after a major update to the on-premises installation process was introduced. The guide includes some legacy information that I reviewed and edited for accuracy and style guide standards.
